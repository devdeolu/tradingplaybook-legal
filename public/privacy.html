Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Trading Playbook ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Trading Playbook mobile application (the "App"). Please read this policy carefully before using the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Account details: your name and email address when you create an account or contact support.
  • Journal data: trades you log in the App (e.g. instrument, direction, entry price, stop loss, take profit, result, screenshots, notes, and checklists). This data is stored on your device and synced to our servers.

1.2 Broker account data (MT4 / MT5 connections)

When you connect a MetaTrader 4 or MetaTrader 5 account, we access and import the following data from your broker account:

  • Account number, account name, account currency, and balance/equity.
  • Trade history: symbol (instrument), trade direction (buy/sell), open and close time, open and close price, lot size, stop loss, take profit, swap, commission, profit/loss, and trade comment.
  • Open positions at the time of sync.

This data is used solely to populate your trade journal and analytics dashboard within the App. Broker connections are established via MetaApi, a third-party cloud service — your broker server address and account credentials are transmitted to MetaApi to maintain the connection (see Section 3). We do not store your broker login password on our own servers.

1.3 Subscription and purchase data

When you subscribe to a paid plan or make an in-app purchase, we receive transaction information from RevenueCat (our subscription management provider), including:

  • Your subscription plan and status (active, expired, cancelled).
  • Purchase and renewal dates, transaction identifiers, and price paid.
  • Whether you are in a free trial or promotional offer period.

We do not receive or store your payment card details — all payment processing is handled by Apple's App Store or Google Play.

1.4 Automatically collected information

  • Usage analytics: anonymised information about how you use the App (e.g. screens visited, features used), collected via Firebase Analytics (Google LLC).
  • Crash and performance data: error reports and diagnostics collected via Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC) to help us fix bugs and improve stability.
  • Device information: device model, OS version, and anonymous identifiers provided by Apple or Google.
  • Advertising identifiers: We do not collect Apple's Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) or Google's Advertising ID. The App does not display third-party advertisements.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes and on the following legal bases:

  • To provide and operate the App (trade journal, analytics, coaching tools, MT4/MT5 sync) — performance of our contract with you.
  • To manage your subscription and in-app purchases, including verifying entitlements and processing access to premium features — performance of our contract with you.
  • To improve the App through analytics, diagnostics, and crash reporting — our legitimate interests in maintaining and improving a reliable product.
  • To respond to your support requests and communications — our legitimate interests and your request.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations — legal compliance.
  • To send you optional product updates or communications (only where you have given consent) — your consent, which you may withdraw at any time.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate and improve the App:

  • Firebase Analytics & Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC) — usage analytics and crash reporting. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Sentry (Functional Software, Inc.) — error tracking and performance monitoring. Error payloads sent to Sentry may include device information, app state, and user context (such as a user identifier) to help us diagnose issues. Subject to Sentry's Privacy Policy.
  • MetaApi (MetaApi Ltd) — broker connectivity for MT4/MT5 account syncing. When you connect a MetaTrader account, your broker server address and account credentials are transmitted to MetaApi's cloud infrastructure to establish and maintain the connection. MetaApi processes this data on our behalf to import your trade history and account information. Subject to MetaApi's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI (OpenAI, L.L.C.) — AI-powered trade coaching and analysis. When you use the AI Coach feature, selected trade data (such as trade details, journal notes, and performance metrics) is sent to OpenAI's API for analysis. OpenAI processes this data under their API data usage policy, which states that API inputs are not used to train their models. Subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — subscription and in-app purchase management. Subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Billing — payment processing for subscriptions and in-app purchases. Subject to Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies.
  • Email and support tools — to manage communication with you.

These services process data on our behalf in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable law. We do not sell your data to these or any other third parties.

4. Affiliate Links and Referrals

The App and/or the Trading Playbook website may contain affiliate links to third-party products or services (e.g. brokers, prop firms, trading tools, educational courses). If you click an affiliate link and make a purchase or sign up, we may receive a commission or referral fee at no additional cost to you.

  • Affiliate partners may use cookies, tracking pixels, or similar technologies to attribute referrals. These are governed by the affiliate partner's own privacy policy.
  • We do not share your personal information with affiliate partners. The affiliate relationship is based solely on the referral link you clicked.
  • Affiliate links do not influence the content, features, or recommendations within the App. We only partner with services we believe are relevant to traders.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

On the Trading Playbook website (tradingplaybook.io)

Our website may use the following technologies:

  • Essential cookies: required for the website to function correctly (e.g. remembering your preferences). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: used to understand how visitors interact with our website (e.g. pages visited, time on site). We may use tools such as Google Analytics for this purpose.
  • Affiliate tracking cookies: placed by affiliate partners when you click an affiliate link, used to attribute referrals and calculate commissions. These are third-party cookies governed by the affiliate partner's privacy policy.

You can manage or block cookies through your browser settings. Disabling non-essential cookies will not affect core website functionality.

In the Trading Playbook app

The App does not use browser cookies. However, Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics may use device-level identifiers and local storage to collect usage and diagnostic data as described in Section 1.4 above.

6. Data Storage and Retention

  • On-device data: Your trade journal and notes are stored locally on your device for offline access. Deleting the App removes locally cached data from your device.
  • Server data: All accounts (Free and Pro) sync journal and account data to our secure servers. You may permanently delete your account and all associated server-side data at any time from within the App: Settings → Delete Account. Account data will be fully removed from our servers within 30 days of your deletion request.
  • Analytics & crash logs: Usage analytics are retained for up to 12 months. Crash reports are retained for up to 6 months. Both are anonymised or pseudonymised where possible.
  • Support communications: Records of email support are retained for up to 2 years unless you request earlier deletion.

7. Data Security and Breach Notification

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and access controls on our servers. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

In the event of an eligible data breach that is likely to cause serious harm, we will notify affected individuals and the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) as required under Australia's Notifiable Data Breaches (NDB) scheme (Privacy Act 1988). Where we also have obligations under GDPR or other applicable law, we will comply with the most stringent applicable notification requirement.

8. Your Rights and Choices

General rights (all users)

  • You can control what you log in the App and may delete journal entries at any time from within the App.
  • You may opt out of certain analytics via device-level settings on iOS (Settings → Privacy → Tracking) or Android.
  • All signed-up users may permanently delete their account and all server-stored data via Settings → Delete Account inside the App.
  • For further assistance with account or data deletion, contact us at support@tradingplaybook.io.

EU / UK users — your GDPR rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK GDPR:

  • Right of access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure: request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restrict processing: request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability: request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent: where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing.
  • Right to lodge a complaint: you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority (e.g. the ICO in the UK, or your national DPA in the EU).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@tradingplaybook.io. We will respond within 30 days.

California users — CCPA

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to request deletion, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information for commercial purposes. To exercise your rights or ask questions about how we handle your data, contact us at support@tradingplaybook.io.
