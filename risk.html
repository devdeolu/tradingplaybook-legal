Leverage Risk

Many instruments available in forex and CFD markets are traded on margin, meaning that a small movement in price can result in gains or losses that are a multiple of your initial deposit. Leverage amplifies both profits and losses. You can lose more than your initial deposit when trading on leverage. Ensure you fully understand how margin and leverage work before trading with borrowed funds.

Market Volatility

Financial markets can experience extreme and sudden volatility, including price gaps, flash crashes, liquidity crises, and unexpected geopolitical or macroeconomic events. These conditions can cause you to lose far more than anticipated, even if you have a stop-loss in place, as execution at your desired price is not always guaranteed.
